description: Research a company as a potential employer
arguments:
  - name: company
    description: Company name to research
    required: true
---

Use the company-researcher agent to conduct a thorough analysis of $ARGUMENTS.company as a potential employer. Generate a comprehensive research report covering:

- Company overview and mission
- Products/services and market position
- Company culture and values
- Remote work policies
- Recent news and developments
- Current job openings relevant to the user's profile
- Fit assessment based on user context

Save the report to `outputs/analysis/company-reports/`.
